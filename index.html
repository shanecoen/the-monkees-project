The Monkees will very soon be announcing the tour dates for the much anticipated 50th anniversary tour.

The Monkees TV Series Soon To Released On Blu-Ray

We're excited to reveal the contents of this long-awaited collection.

All 58 original, uncut episodes of THE MONKEES, remastered in HD from the original film negatives including Audio commentary from all four Monkees.

Includes never-before-seen out-takes and comes with unique packaging and details of how to purchase will be released very soon!!

Daydream Believer Named The Greatest

Rolling Stone Magazine recently ran a Poll to find out the greatest Monkees song of all time.

Folling a huge fan vote the hit song "Daydream Believer" came in at number one beating off close competition from "I'm a Believer" and "Last train To Clarkesville"! You can check out the hit song here...

The Movie "Head" To get Cinema Release

The Monkees 1968 Movie "Head" is to get a new limited cinema release as part of the 50th anniversary celebrations.

The movie which was famously produced by Jack Nicholson, who also made a cameo appearance along with Dennis Hopper, will be screened in selected cinemas this coming June!

Stay tuned for full details of all dates and venues!!!
