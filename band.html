The Monkees Are Born

In 1965 the search began for 4 young men to star in a new TV series with the plan of riding on the coat-tails of The Beatles phenomenon but who would have guessed that 50 years after its premiere (and two running seasons) that The Monkees would remain as popular as ever. The Monkees was a light-hearted sitcom following the bands exploits that featured early incarnations of music videos and plenty of zanny antics. The show ran from 1966 to 1968 but gained plenty of new fans in the 1980s with the MTV generation. In all 58 episodes were aired and The Monkees rock n roll legend was born!
