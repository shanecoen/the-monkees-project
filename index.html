Micky Dolenz

Born in Los Angeles on March 8th 1948.

Micky first established himself as a performer at age 10 when, under the stage name of Micky Braddock, he starred in his first TV series, Circus Boy, which aired on NBC from 1956-1958.

In his teens, Micky guest-starred on a number of television shows and also learned to play guitar.

Micky has spent much of his recent years acting and directing working with such shows as The Drew Carey Show and Days Of Our Lives and has also enjoyed time working in Broadway.

David Jones

Born in Manchester on December 30th 1945.

His television acting debut was on the British television soap opera Coronation Street where he appeared for just one episode in 1961.

Davy spent some of his youth as a jockey before taking up performing full time.

While working on The Monkees TV show he attracted a lot of attention from fans for his charm, wit, warm sense of humor and boyish good looks. He sang lead vocals on many of the Monkees' recordings and was known as a good drummer.
